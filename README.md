# Joint Replacement Recovery Zone (JRRZ)

The Joint Replacement Recovery Zone is a patient-centered framework designed to help individuals prepare for, recover from, and thrive after joint replacement surgery.

This project provides structured guidance for the recovery journey by combining patient experience with clinically aligned recovery frameworks.

The goal is to make recovery understandable, navigable, and successful.

---

## Core Concept

Joint replacement recovery is a **structured transition between states of mobility**.

JRRZ models recovery as a series of phases:

1. Decision & Preparation
2. Surgery & Immediate Recovery
3. Functional Recovery
4. Strength & Stability
5. Longevity & Joint Protection

Each phase includes practical guidance, expected milestones, and recovery strategies.

---

## Project Structure

The Joint Replacement Recovery Zone contains several specialized domains:

- Knee Zone
- Hip Zone
- Future joint-specific recovery zones

These zones provide focused guidance tailored to each type of joint replacement.

---

## Purpose

Most patients receive fragmented information about recovery. JRRZ provides a coherent recovery roadmap to help individuals understand:

- what to expect
- how recovery progresses
- how to avoid setbacks
- how to regain mobility

---

## Long-Term Vision

The JRRZ framework may evolve into a collaborative platform that integrates:

- patient experiences
- surgeon insights
- physical therapy best practices
- structured recovery milestones

The ultimate goal is to improve patient outcomes and confidence throughout the recovery process.
