Patient Recovery Roadmap

The Joint Replacement Recovery Zone is designed to help patients understand the journey before and after joint replacement surgery.

Phase 1: Preparation

  • Understand the surgical plan
  • Prepare the home for safer mobility
  • Arrange support after surgery
  • Set realistic expectations

Phase 2: Surgery

  • Expect temporary disruption
  • Focus on safety and basic movement
  • Follow discharge instructions

Phase 3: Early Recovery

  • Begin walking and movement
  • Follow therapy guidance
  • Track small improvements

Phase 4: Functional Recovery

  • Improve walking mechanics
  • Work on stairs and daily activities
  • Focus on movement quality

Phase 5: Strength and Stability

  • Rebuild muscle strength
  • Improve balance
  • Increase endurance

Phase 6: Long-Term Joint Health

  • Maintain mobility habits
  • Protect the joint
  • Address concerns early
